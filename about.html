About

Who We Are

O.R. TRAX is an electronic, medical standard database allowing healthcare providers the ability to send surgical schedules to vendor representatives who supply products such as implants, instrumentation, and support to their operating rooms. Free of PHI, and only accessible to vendors credentialed at your facility, O.R. TRAX's database was developed to restore time to healthcare professionals, strengthen HIPAA compliance, and reduce O.R. expenses resulting from vendor tardiness.

OR TRAX delivers an online database bridging the communication of surgical schedules between healthcare providers and vendor representatives. HIPAA compliant and available from any web accessible device, OR TRAX boosts efficiency for both healthcare providers and vendors when conferring surgical schedule information.

Clinical & Vendor Case Studies

In recent years, the healthcare industry has absorbed immense regulations designed to increase the safety and privacy of healthcare providers and their patients. OR TRAX enhances the ability for providers to seize control of patient security by delivering vendors a reference database free of protected health information (PHI).

Once healthcare providers mandate OR TRAX as the sole method of surgical schedule procurement by vendors, their team-members will be free from concern of such topics as outlined in case studies below.
